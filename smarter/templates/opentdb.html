Open Trivia Database

Open Trivia Database (OpenTDB) provides a substantial amount of questions for Smarter. It contains more than 4000 verified questions of varying difficulties and categories. It is not possible to contribute to the database directly through Smarter, although you are welcome to do so on their site.
