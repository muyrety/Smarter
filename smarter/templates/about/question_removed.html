Why was my question removed?

Question removal can happen due to various reasons. First of all, if you have recently submitted a new question, it is very likely that it has been rejected. Make sure your submission meets these criteria:

  • Similar question has not been submitted before. You can check questions submitted by users here .
  • Your question has the right category and difficulty selected. Make sure to go through all categories and difficulty levels to decide which one best suits your question.
  • The answers to your question are factually correct. Check if the correct answer is definitely correct and incorrect answers are all unambiguously incorrect.
  • The answer fields are filled correctly. For example, if a question has 3 incorrect answers, make sure they are all different.

On the other hand, if your question was accepted and then deleted afterwards, it may have been overlooked and its flaws detected only after submission. Again, make sure it met all the aforementioned requirements.
